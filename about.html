About VUT Eats

Our Mission

At VUT Eats, we believe that great food is essential for a great mind. Our mission is to provide the Vaal University of Technology community with fresh, delicious, and affordable food options to fuel your studies, work, and campus life.

Our Food

We are passionate about quality. From quick-grab snacks and barista-made coffee to hearty lunches and healthy options, we source fresh ingredients daily. We proudly offer a variety of choices, including vegetarian, vegan, and halaal options.

Opening Hours

  • Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Sunday & Public Holidays: Closed
